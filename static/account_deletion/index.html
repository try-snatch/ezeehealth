EzeeHealth BridgeCare

If you would like to request the deletion of your EzeeHealth BridgeCare account and all associated data, please send an email to:

support@ezeehealth.in

Please include the following in your email:

  • 1. Your registered phone number or email address
  • 2. Your full name as registered on the app
  • 3. Reason for account deletion (optional)

What happens when your account is deleted:

  • • Your profile information (name, phone, email) will be permanently deleted
  • • All uploaded documents and medical records will be removed
  • • Your patient referral history will be anonymized
  • • This action is irreversible and cannot be undone

We will process your request within 7 business days and send a confirmation to your registered email address.
